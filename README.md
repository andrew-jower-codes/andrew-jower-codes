
# About Me

My name is Andrew Jower, and I am a fourth-year student at UC Davis pursuing a double major in Science & Technology Studies and Sociology: Organizational Studies.

In addition to serving as president of the STS Club at UC Davis, I provide *pro bono* data analysis support to local nonprofits. I am interested in understanding how disruptive technology affects society as well as how artificial intelligence can be ethically incorporated into private, nonprofit, and government organizations.

I am always happy to connect with others interested in similar work. For collaboration inquiries, feel free to reach out at andrewjower@gmail.com.
